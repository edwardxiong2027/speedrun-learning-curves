## Top 5 Unique Research Ideas

### 1. The Mathematics of Speedrunning: Analyzing Human Learning Curves and Optimization Strategies in Video Games
**Data Source:** Speedrun.com API (millions of recorded runs)
**Why It's Unique:** No academic papers have analyzed speedrunning from a mathematical/physics perspective
**Research Questions:**
- Do speedrun times follow predictable mathematical decay curves?
- Can we predict when a world record will be broken?
- How do different game mechanics affect optimization limits?
**Web App:** Interactive visualization of speedrun progression over time
**Fun Factor:** Very high - appeals to gaming community

---

### 2. Predicting Aurora Borealis Visibility Using Machine Learning and Solar Wind Data
**Data Source:** NASA DSCOVR satellite data, NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center
**Why It's Unique:** Existing aurora forecasts are crude; ML approach is novel
**Research Questions:**
- Can we improve aurora prediction accuracy using ML on solar wind parameters?
- What features (speed, density, magnetic field) are most predictive?
**Web App:** Real-time aurora visibility predictor for any location
**Fun Factor:** High - beautiful natural phenomenon, practical for aurora chasers

---

### 3. Analyzing the Decay of Internet Content: A Mathematical Model of "Link Rot"
**Data Source:** Internet Archive Wayback Machine, Common Crawl dataset
**Why It's Unique:** Critical problem for digital preservation, understudied
**Research Questions:**
- What is the half-life of web content?
- Can we predict which websites are at risk of disappearing?
- How does link rot affect academic citations?
**Web App:** "Link Health Checker" tool
**Fun Factor:** Medium-High - reveals hidden fragility of internet

---

### 4. The Network Science of Chess: Predicting Game Outcomes from Opening Theory Evolution
**Data Source:** Lichess.org database (5+ billion games, fully open)
**Why It's Unique:** Massive dataset rarely used for ML research by students
**Research Questions:**
- How has opening theory evolved over the past 10 years?
- Can ML predict game outcomes from the first 5 moves?
- Which openings have the highest "surprise factor"?
**Web App:** Chess opening analyzer with win probability
**Fun Factor:** Very High - chess is popular, visual results

---

### 5. Using Machine Learning to Predict Wildfire Spread Patterns
**Data Source:** NIFC (National Interagency Fire Center), NOAA weather data
**Why It's Unique:** Highly relevant to California, critical societal impact
**Research Questions:**
- Which weather/terrain features best predict fire spread?
- Can we improve early warning systems with ML?
**Web App:** Fire risk predictor based on current conditions
**Fun Factor:** Medium - serious topic but high impact

## JEI Requirements Checklist
- [ ] Must have hypothesis-driven research
- [ ] Must use their manuscript template
- [ ] Adult mentor must submit (not student)
- [ ] $45 submission fee
- [ ] Required sections: Summary, Introduction, Results, Discussion, Materials/Methods, References
- [ ] No literature reviews or invention descriptions
